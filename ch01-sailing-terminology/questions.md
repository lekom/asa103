# Chapter 1: Sailing Terminology

## Questions

### Q1
What is the **bow** of a boat?
- A) The rear of the boat
- B) The front of the boat
- C) The left side when facing forward
- D) The right side when facing forward

### Q2
Which term describes the right side of a boat when facing forward?
- A) Port
- B) Bow
- C) Starboard
- D) Stern

### Q3
What is the **beam** of a boat?
- A) The length from bow to stern
- B) The widest point of the hull
- C) The depth of the keel
- D) The height of the mast

### Q4
What does **draft** refer to?
- A) The height of the mast above the waterline
- B) The distance from the waterline to the bottom of the keel
- C) The width of the hull at its widest point
- D) The length of the boat at the waterline

### Q5
What is the **helm**?
- A) The top of the mast
- B) The keel of the boat
- C) The steering mechanism (wheel or tiller)
- D) The anchor locker

### Q6
Which term refers to the left side of the boat when facing forward?
- A) Starboard
- B) Leeward
- C) Windward
- D) Port

### Q7
What is the **freeboard**?
- A) The distance from the waterline to the deck
- B) The distance from the keel to the waterline
- C) A free section of the deck with no equipment
- D) The length of the bowsprit

### Q8
What does **LOA** stand for?
- A) Length on Approach
- B) Length Over All
- C) Lateral Orientation Angle
- D) Leeward Offset Adjustment

### Q9
What is the **cockpit**?
- A) The area below deck where the crew sleeps
- B) The enclosed area at the bow
- C) The open area from which the boat is steered and managed
- D) The compartment housing the engine

### Q10
What is the purpose of **stanchions**?
- A) To support the boom
- B) To support the lifelines around the deck
- C) To secure the anchor
- D) To hold the sails in place

### Q11
What does **abeam** mean?
- A) Directly ahead
- B) Directly behind
- C) At right angles to the centerline of the boat
- D) At a 45-degree angle off the bow

### Q12
What is a **bulkhead**?
- A) A wall or partition below deck
- B) The reinforced bow of the boat
- C) The cap at the top of the mast
- D) A type of heavy anchor

### Q13
What is the **transom**?
- A) The forward-most part of the bow
- B) The flat surface at the stern of the boat
- C) The opening in the deck for the mast
- D) A crossbeam supporting the mast

### Q14
What is the **companionway**?
- A) The passageway between berths
- B) The entrance/stairway from the cockpit to the cabin below
- C) The walkway along the side deck
- D) The ladder up to the mast

### Q15
What does **windward** mean?
- A) The direction the wind is blowing toward
- B) The direction from which the wind is blowing (toward the wind)
- C) A calm area with no wind
- D) Perpendicular to the wind direction

1. B
2. C
3. B
4. B
5. C
6. D
7. A
8. B
9. C
10. B
11. C
12. A
13. B
