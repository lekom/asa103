# Chapter 5: Weather

## Questions

- B) Approaching storm or deteriorating conditions

- B) Nimbostratus

- B) Wind blowing from the sea toward the land during the day

- B) Wind blowing from the land toward the sea, typically at night

- B) Moderate breeze
- C) Strong breeze / near gale conditions

- A) A warm front is passing; conditions are deteriorating
- B) A cold front has passed; conditions are generally improving
- C) A hurricane is approaching
- D) Fog is developing

- B) A wind that shifts counterclockwise (in the Northern Hemisphere)
- C) A wind that increases in speed
- D) A wind that blows from directly astern

- B) A line of dark, towering cumulonimbus clouds

- B) Fair weather that will last for days

- B) Cold, dry air moves over warm water
