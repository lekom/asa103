# Chapter 2: Sail Theory & Trim

## Questions

- B) Lift generated by airflow over the curved sail shape
- C) The weight of the boat moving downhill on waves

- B) The leading edge of the sail is fluttering or shaking

- A) Measuring wind speed
- B) Indicating proper airflow over the sails for trim
- C) Showing the depth of water

- A) Ease the jib sheet

- C) Head up (turn toward the wind)

### Q12

- B) A wind shift that forces you to fall off (bear away)

### Q13

- B) A wind shift that allows you to point higher toward your destination
- C) The upward force on the keel

### Q14

- B) The wind felt on the boat, combining true wind and the boat's motion
- C) The wind at the top of the mast
- D) The forecast wind speed

- B) The difference in angle of attack between the top and bottom of the sail
